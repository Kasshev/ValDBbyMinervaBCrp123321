The EAPSF (as captured during one of their operations)

Name: The Eastern Armed Private Security Firm
Type: A private military company
Status: No longer standalone
Relationship: Part of AEGIS Corporation
The Eastern Armed Private Security Firm is a PMC founded in eastern Russia. They specialize in sending out their operators assited by teams of armed robots and drones. Their advanced technology gave them popularity among their clients. Low risks of death and other injuries make it a popular choice for mercenaries that wish to have a stable job. After operation "Raid" the EAPSF has been acquired by AEGIS Corporation
